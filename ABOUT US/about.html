About Online Quiz System

Enhancing Learning Through Technology

The Online Quiz System web-based application designed to manage student and teacher interactions.

The system provides separate login pages for students and teachers, with various functionalities for teachers to manage subjects, questions, and student registrations.create, attempt, and evaluate quizzes in real-time.

Key features include:

    Teacher dashboard

  • ✔️ Separate login pages for students and teachers.
  • ✔️ Teacher login requires sign-up and sign-in.
  • ✔️ Total count of teachers, students, and subjects.
  • ✔️ Create, show, update, and delete subjects.
  • ✔️ Create, update, and delete questions.
  • ✔️ Data stored in local storage.
  • ✔️ Student Registration.
  • ✔️ Registration form for teachers to register students
  • ✔️ Requirements: name, father name, DOB, mobile, enrollment, password, address Data displayed in a table
  • ✔️ Search results for specific subjects Certificate appears on dynamic data
  • ✔️ Secure logout for teachers and students
  • ✔️ Front-end: HTML, CSS,Bootstrap, JavaScript

    - Database: Local Storage

    • Student Login Form

  • ✔️ Choose Enrollment and Password
  • ✔️ Guidelines for Attempting Quiz
  • ✔️ Choose Subject for Quiz Students
  • ✔️ Questions entered by the teacher in the dashboard appear for the student to attempt.
  • ✔️ Submit Quiz
  • ✔️ Students submit the quiz, and the results are stored in local storage.
